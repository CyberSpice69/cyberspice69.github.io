Domme Ewan is a neuro-queer Artist, Designer, Performer, and Educator based in Glasgow, Scotland.
They use creative applications of technology to empower people, and encourage dialogues concerning the impacts of tech on people and our society.
Approaching digital tools in a hands-on manner; Domme blurs the conventional boundaries of interface, whilst considering the intangible and ephemeral properties of digital materials, techniques and spaces.
